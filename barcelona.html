Barcelona.


Barcelona is a city on the coast of northeastern Spain.

It is the capital and largest city of the autonomous community of Catalonia, as well as the second most populous municipality of Spain.

With a population of 1.6 million within city limits, its urban area extends to numerous neighbouring municipalities within the province of Barcelona and is home to around 4.8 million people.

It is one of the largest metropolises on the Meditteranean Sea, located on the coast between the mouths of the rivers Llobregat and Besos and founded to the west by the Serra de Collserola mountain range.
